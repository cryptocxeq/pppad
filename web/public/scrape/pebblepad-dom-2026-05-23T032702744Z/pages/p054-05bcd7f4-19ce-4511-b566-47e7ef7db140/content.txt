Unit 6 Step 5
Reflections  on Effective Teaching and Learning Strategies.
Learning Outcomes:
Evaluate their own personal values, beliefs and experiences and consider the implications of these in their roles as educators
Articulate a critical engagement with literature relating to a variety of contemporary, best practice pedagogical approaches used internationally and their contexts, recognising their potential implications for learning and teaching.
Activities
1. Reflect on the teaching and learning styles you have seen used by other teachers in your school or setting.
Can you identify aspects from the readings and videos you have watched?
2. Arguably these aspects should feature in any short sequence of learning:  Challenge, Explanation, Modelling, Practice, Questioning and Feedback. Are there scenarios where we need more or less than this approach?
3. Can you still be a good teacher without engaging with education literature?
4. How do experienced teachers feel about these new ideas of effective teaching?
5. Is there one best way for everyone to teach?
Make some notes on your thought and critical comments. Use this information to focus your engagement with the assignment
