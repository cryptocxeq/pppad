Unit 4 Step 1
Introduction to Behaviour Management
In unit 4, we learn about some of the approaches to behaviour management to enhance pupils' learning.
Learning Outcomes:
Articulate a critical engagement with literature relating to a variety of contemporary, best practice international primary/secondary pedagogical approaches and frameworks, including the primary/secondary/ Foundation Stage, and their contexts,
Evidence a critical understanding of the ways in which the policy, cultural and social contexts of education and of learners’ lives can influence their learning, behaviours and wellbeing​​​​​​​.
