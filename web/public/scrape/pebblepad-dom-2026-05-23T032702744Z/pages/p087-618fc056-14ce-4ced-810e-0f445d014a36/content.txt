Critical writing
Thinking and writing critically involves asking  the question: "What do I  think about this  - and why?"  It means thinking analytically in order to explain how much you agree or disagree with something: it involves assessing the accuracy, usefulness and validity of what you read, hear or see.
The aim of this workbook is to help you to develop critical thinking, reading and writing skills
