Reflection on Pupil Development
Learning Objective
 Demonstrate a critical, evidence-based understanding of learner development.
Activities
1. Reflect on a pupil in one of your classes/ sessions. Thinking about the learning theory and the nature and nurture reading you have done, consider what has influenced that pupil’s learning. Consider the family, school, societal influences that influence their learning.
Give the pupil a pseudonym and write a 200- 300 word profile of the influences on the success of that pupil’s learning.
Identify areas where you may need to read to support you discussion for your presentation. ​​​​​​​
2. Reflect on the learning outcome of this unit and identify one thing you have learnt.
3. Identify any questions you have about this step of the unit on the ongoing Pebble pad on the end tab.
