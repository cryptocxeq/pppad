University Services
The university services are available to you as a student.
1. The library has a librarian especially for education. Go to this page for a set of resources. The library has a set of study skills workshops you can do.
2.  There are student advisors if you get behind, have financial difficulties and need to defer or if you need to know your  rights or the regulations.  At student.adviser@uwe.ac.uk
 3. The University has a disability service if you have issues with studying.  They can support your learning. Contact the disability services through infopoint@uwe.ac.uk
4. The university careers service is open to you and can advise on international careers infopoint@uwe.ac.uk
5. There is an education society you can join that has online meetings as well as live. Follow them on Instagram @uweeducationsociety.
6. Siamak can provide technical support if you are having difficulties accessing materials etc. He is fabulous and never makes you feel stupid! Siamak.Alimi@uwe.ac.uk
