Unit 3 Step 2
My Curriculum
Learning Outcomes
Analyse and articulate a developed understanding of the purposes, forms and impact of local curricula.
Articulate a critical engagement with literature relating to a variety of contemporary, best practice international primary/ secondary pedagogical approaches and frameworks, including NC, and their contexts, recognising their potential implications for learning and teaching in international contexts.
Activity
1. In this section, we want you to start thinking about  the curriculum and how it is shaped. This activity will help you to start analysing curricula. Think back to a stage of your own schooling .
For a chosen age group, specify:
A. the subjects/topics studied
B. choices of subjects/topics offered to pupils
C. time spent on other subjects
D. additional activities
Using the framework modelled by Ross, (2000) below, identify forms of organisation you believe your curriculum to have had. It may be that your curriculum drew on all forms or maybe just one. Link your judgements with examples.
Bring your notes to the live discussion.
