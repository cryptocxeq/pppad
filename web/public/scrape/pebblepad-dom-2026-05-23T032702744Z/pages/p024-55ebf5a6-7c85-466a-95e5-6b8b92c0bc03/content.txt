Unit 1  Step 5
Observing Teaching and Learning
Learning Outcome
Develop students’ critical understanding of a range of theories and practices of learning and teaching to support all learners to make good progress.
Activity
In this unit, we have been reflecting on values and how this impacts on our approach to teaching. To get you to start to focus on the values of other teachers and pupils' learning we are going to get you to do some observation of teaching and learning.
The activity will prepare you for the next unit on the developing pupil and to start thinking about reflecting on your own teaching.
The video below introduces the focused observation.
Choose one of the observation sheets and carry out an observation of a single pupil for about 30 mins in a colleague's lesson or monitor a pupil in your own lesson. You don't have to interact with the pupil. Please ask the teacher's permission before carrying this out. Respond to the focus questions below.
1.What values do you think the teacher holds from your observation? What is your evidence for this?
1.What values do you think the teacher holds from your observation? What is your evidence for this? multi-line text editor.
2. When you observed the pupils, what did you notice about aspects of teaching that helped them learn? What stopped them learning?
2. When you observed the pupils, what did you notice about aspects of teaching that helped them learn? What stopped them learning? multi-line text editor.

What links can they make to other work they have done?
