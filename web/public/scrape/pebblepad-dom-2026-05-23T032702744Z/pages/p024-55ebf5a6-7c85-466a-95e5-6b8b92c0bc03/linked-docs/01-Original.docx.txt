Pupil L earning Observation . Choose one of your own lessons/sessions and observe one pupil every 2-3 minutes. Record what they are doing. At the end ask the pupil some questions listed below. Record their answers. Learning Outcome of Lesson: Age of Pupil: Subject/Topic Task Time Pupil’s actions e.g. Teacher explanation 9.10 Listening but not putting up hand Pupil perception on Task End of lesson Answer What did the pupil think they were supposed to be learning? What did they think they learnt/ practiced/ experienced? Why did they think they were learning the content? Is there anything the teacher could have done to make the lesson clearer/ easier? What links can they make to other work they have done?
