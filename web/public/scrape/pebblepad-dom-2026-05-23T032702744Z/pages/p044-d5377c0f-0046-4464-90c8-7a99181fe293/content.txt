Unit 5 Step 1
Introduction to Effective Learning
The next two units explore current thinking on effective teaching and learning strategies. They will look at current theories, critiques of theories and ask you to reflect on strategies you use.
Learning Outcomes
Articulate a critical engagement with literature relating to a variety of contemporary, best practice pedagogical approaches used internationally and their contexts, recognising their potential implications for learning and teaching.
