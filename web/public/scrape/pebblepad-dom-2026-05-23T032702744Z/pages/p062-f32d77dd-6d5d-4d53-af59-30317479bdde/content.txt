Unit 8 Step 1
Introduction to SEND
Unit 8 explores special educational needs and disability (SEND) and how we include all pupils regardless of their culture, race, disabilities, gender, sexuality etc. It reviews the terms we use, our attitudes to SEND and how we can provide access to the curriculum for pupils.
Learning Outcomes
Evaluate their own personal values, beliefs and experiences and consider the implications of these in their roles as educators.
Critically explore a range of philosophies of education and evidence of the potential impact of these on educational provision and the experiences of learners in different contexts.
Evidence a critical understanding of the ways in which the policy, cultural and social contexts of education and of learners’ lives can influence their learning, behaviours and wellbeing
Activities
The video lecture content includes:
Defining educational need.
Evaluation on perspectives of addressing special educational needs.
Inclusion and strategies for reducing barriers to achievement.
Watch the lecture, pausing at appropriate points to make notes and to reflect on your practice and your role as teacher. Make notes below.
