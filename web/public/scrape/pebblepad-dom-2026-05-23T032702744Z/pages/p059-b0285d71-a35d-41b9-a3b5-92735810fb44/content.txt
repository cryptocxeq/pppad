Unit 7 Step 4
Reflecting on Assessment Strategies
Learning Outcomes
Critically explore a range of philosophies of education and evidence of the potential impact of these on educational provision and the experiences of learners in different contexts.
Group  Activity
The purpose of the activity is to evaluate the practices you have been studying and thinking about their relevance to your own context. The session will allow students to share good practice with each other.
Describe the strategies you have watched to your primary/ secondary fellow colleagues at school.
Discuss the strategies and identify whether you think they could/could not work in your setting.
Note down any ideas you had during the discussion.
What Assessment Strategies have you seen or used? What strategies would you like to try?
