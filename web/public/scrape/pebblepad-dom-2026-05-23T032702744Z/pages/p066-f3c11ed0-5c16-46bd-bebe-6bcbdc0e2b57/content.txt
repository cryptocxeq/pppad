Unit 8 Step 5
Reflecting and Extending Knowledge on SEND.
Learning Outcomes
Evaluate their own personal values, beliefs and experiences and consider the implications of these in their roles as educators.
Activities
1.Talk to your class teacher or mentor about how they address the needs of all children in their planning, teaching and assessing:. Some aspects you might consider:
How do they adapt their teaching?
What scaffolding do they employ?
Do they use different resources?
How do they deploy support staff?
Do they alter the environment and how?
How do they cater for neurodiversity?
